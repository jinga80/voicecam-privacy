📞 Auto-backup call recordings

Are your call recordings
actually being backed up?

Phone call recordings — most of them are never backed up automatically.

All those call recordings piling up on your phone vanish the moment you lose the device. VoiceCam lets you pick your call-recording folder directly and auto-backs up each new call to your own drive. No 'all files access' permission needed — only the folder you choose, kept safe.

  • Pick the call-recording folder → new calls back up automatically
  • Back up all your existing calls in one go too
  • Wi-Fi-only option · tidy up after upload
🌙 Sleep analysis

While you sleep,
read the signals your body sends

Record in sleep mode and VoiceCam analyzes the amplitude on-device to find your snoring score and suspected apnea segments. You can jump straight to any suspicious moment and listen from that point.

  • Snoring score · count of suspected apnea events
  • Overnight intensity timeline + event markers
  • Useful as reference for an ENT or sleep-clinic consultation
🆘 Emergency SOS

In a critical moment,
it makes the call for you

If a dangerous loud sound is detected during recording, a full-screen alert appears, and if you don't cancel it, VoiceCam automatically calls your chosen contact. Even on nights you sleep alone, even outdoors.

  • Detects shouting and danger sounds automatically (on-device)
  • Cancel countdown → automatic call
  • Set the sensitivity and contact yourself

  • Auto-segments wherever the talking pauses
  • Auto-marks important parts · skip quiet stretches as you listen
  • Browse by date · notes · search
  • Add a password before sharing, kept safe
